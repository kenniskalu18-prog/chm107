Lab Report Format

Reports must follow the manual format with these sections:

  1. Date of experiment
  2. Title of experiment
  3. Aim of experiment
  4. Apparatus used
  5. Results and calculations
  6. Conclusion

Lab Notebook Requirements:

  • Complete all necessary info: Name, Matric No., Department, Level, Group/Batch Lab
  • Treat as examination booklet
  • No tearing, no tapes
  • Cross out errors neatly

Safety Rules & Chemical Handling

Why lab safety is important: To prevent accidents and ensure a secure learning environment.

Chemical Handling Rules:

  • Always read labels carefully
  • Never use unlabelled chemicals
  • Use a spatula or scoopula for solid chemicals
  • Never touch chemicals with bare hands
  • Do not smell or taste chemicals
  • Never use metal spatulas with peroxides
  • Always add acid to water, never water to acid
  • Handle toxic/fume-producing chemicals only in a fume hood

  • Do not use equipment or chemicals you do not understand
  • Never perform unauthorized experiments
  • Never work alone in the laboratory
  • Work only during scheduled laboratory hours

  • Keep flammable materials away from flames
  • Do not leave burners or heaters unattended
  • Store bags and coats in designated areas

Know the locations of:

  • Fire extinguishers
  • Eyewash stations
  • Safety showers
  • Emergency exits

Emergency Preparedness

If an accident occurs:

  1. Call for help immediately
  2. Inform the instructor or technologist
  3. Assist the injured person calmly
  4. Help with first aid if instructed
  5. Evacuate via nearest exit

Important: All injuries, no matter how minor, must be reported immediately.

Safety is everyone's responsibility:

  • Follow instructions carefully
  • Ask questions when unsure
  • Protect yourself and others

Housekeeping Rules

A clean lab is a safe lab:

  • Keep benches and aisles clear
  • Clean your workspace at the end of each session
  • Do not block sinks, drains, exits, or emergency equipment
  • Check equipment for cracks or damage before use
  • Never pour chemical waste into sinks

Dispose of:

  • Chemical waste in labelled containers
  • Broken glass in special glass bins
  • Gloves, paper towels, and weigh boats in trash bins

Additional:

  • Ensure windows and doors are open during work and closed afterward
  • Turn off gas, water, and electrical supplies when finished
